THE ULTIMATE CHALLENGE

RULES TO THE GAME

Each turn, a player repeatedly rolls a die until either a 1 is rolled or the player decides to "pass":

  • If the player rolls a 1, they score nothing and it becomes the next player's turn.
  • If the player rolls any other number, it is added to their turn total and the player's turn continues.
  • If a player chooses to "pass", their turn total is added to their score, and it becomes the next player's turn.

The first player to score 100 or more points wins.
